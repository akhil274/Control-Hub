CONTROLHUB - PARTS LIST
=======================

1.ESP32 Development Board
2.DS3231 RTC Module
3.2-Channel Relay Module 5V
4.Power Supply 5V
5.CR2032 Battery
6.Electrical extension board 

WIRING
======
ESP32 GPIO 21 → RTC SDA
ESP32 GPIO 22 → RTC SCL
ESP32 3.3V → RTC VCC
ESP32 GND → RTC GND

ESP32 GPIO 26 → Relay IN1 (Light)
ESP32 GPIO 27 → Relay IN2 (CO2)
ESP32 VIN → Relay VCC (5V)
ESP32 GND → Relay GND
